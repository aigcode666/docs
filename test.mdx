The **Workspace** is your central hub in AutoCoder.cc. It is divided into four main sections: **Projects**, **Publish**, **Domains**, and **Likes**.

---

## 1. Projects

The **Projects** tab displays all your creative drafts and ongoing work.

<Frame>
  ![Image 68](/images/image-68.png)
</Frame>

Clicking on any project card will take you back into the AutoCoder.cc editor, where you can continue building and refining your application using natural language. Use the `...` menu on the project cards to access quick actions like **Preview**, **Remix** (duplicate), or **Delete**.

---

## 2. Publish

The **Publish** tab provides a comprehensive overview of all your live applications and their current health status.

<Frame>
  ![Image 69](/images/image-69.png)
</Frame>

From the overview page, you can quickly copy your public **Website URLs**, access your **Backend Dashboard**, or generate and download the latest **APK** for your mobile apps.

Clicking on any specific project card will open the **Project Publish** detail page, which is split into two configurations:

### Website Publish

Manage the web presence of your project here.

- **Domain Setup**: Bind custom domains, purchase new ones, or configure your free platform subdomain.
- **Share to SparkVibe**: Toggle this switch to make your project publicly visible in the SparkVibe community, allowing others to view and remix your work.
- **Republish**: Push your latest code changes to the live website.

<Frame>
  ![Image 72](/images/image-72.png)
</Frame>

### Mobile App

Manage the mobile installation packages for your project.

- **Android Test Download**: Click the **Pack** button to generate a QR code. You can scan this code with your Android phone to install and test the app directly.
- **App Store & iOS**: Native submission to the Apple App Store and Google Play is currently under development (Stay Tuned).

<Frame>
  ![Image 73](/images/image-73.png)
</Frame>

<Note>
  **Taking your project offline**: If you need to restrict external access to your live project, click the **Unpublish** button located in the top right corner of the Project Publish page.
</Note>

## 3. Domains

The **Domains** tab allows you to centrally monitor and manage all URLs bound to your projects.

<Frame>
  ![Image 70](/images/image-70.png)
</Frame>

### Domain Status

The top overview cards display the real-time health of your domains based on four categories:

- **🟢 Live**: The domain is active and functioning normally.
- **🟠 Warning**: DNS records have conflicts, or the SSL certificate is expiring soon.
- **🔵 Unpublish**: The domain is bound to a project that has been taken offline.
- **⚫️ Expired**: The domain subscription or SSL certificate has expired.

### Management & Primary Domain (Pro feature)

If you bind multiple domains to a single project, you can designate one as the **Primary Domain** by clicking the **⭐ (Star)** icon in the list. All traffic from other alias domains will automatically 301 redirect to your Primary Domain.

Click the **Setting (Gear)** icon next to any domain to manage renewals, update SSL certificates, or unbind the domain from its current project.

---

## 4. Likes

The **Likes** tab acts as your personal inspiration library. Any project you have liked in the **SparkVibe** community is automatically saved here.

<Frame>
  ![Image 71](/images/image-71.png)
</Frame>

You can revisit these projects at any time. If you want to use a liked project as a template, simply open it and select **Remix** to clone it directly into your own **Projects** tab for further development.
