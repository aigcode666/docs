## Project launch



Launch your project externally, including web deployment and domain configuration.



### Web deployment



<Steps>

<Step title="Click Publish">

Click **Publish** in the top right corner of the editor.

</Step>

<Step title="Select the Web tab">

Select the **Web** tab in the pop-up window.

</Step>

<Step title="Configure your domain">

Configure your domain (**Subdomain** or **Custom Domain**). Optionally, toggle **Share to SparkVibe** to share your project with the community.

</Step>

<Step title="Confirm publication">

Click **Publish** to confirm your deployment.

</Step>

</Steps>



<Frame>

![Image](/images/image-74.png)

</Frame>



After deployment, the system generates two links: the frontend display link and the **Admin Dashboard** link for managing full-stack data.



### Subdomain



All users (including Free plan users) receive an `*.autocoder.cc` **Subdomain**. Enter your desired prefix in the **Subdomain** configuration tab to automatically assign it.

### Custom Domain



<Note>

This feature is exclusive to paid plans (the **Standard** plan supports 1 domain, and the **Pro** plan supports unlimited domains).

</Note>



<Steps>

<Step title="Use your own domain">

In the **Custom Domain** tab of the **Publish** pop-up, enter your third-party domain.

</Step>

<Step title="Configure and verify DNS records">

Log in to your domain provider (e.g., GoDaddy) and add the provided CNAME record to your DNS configuration. Once added, return to AutoCoder.cc and click **Verify** to check the resolution status.

</Step>

<Step title="Secure your domain with an SSL certificate">

To enable HTTPS, you can either paste your certificate content (`CRT` and `KEY`) directly into the text fields, or click **Upload file** to upload your `.key` and `.crt`/`.cer` files.

</Step>

</Steps>

## App launch



Complete app packaging and test distribution.



<Note>

The **App** tab is only displayed when your project includes a mobile application.

</Note>



### App store launch



_Currently, the platform does not support directly publishing the generated app to Google Play or the Apple App Store. You must handle compliance and the submission process manually._



### Test package download (Android / iOS)



If you generated a mobile app, switch to the **App** tab after clicking **Publish**.



_Currently, AutoCoder.cc only supports generating and downloading Android APK test packages for installation testing. iOS test packages (.ipa files) and TestFlight distribution are not supported at this time._
